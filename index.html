A day to give something back

Open Source projects make our lives better every day, but often we don't take the time to contribute back to them. So let's do that - and do it together.

It's time to get involved

Are you just starting out on your journey as an Open Source contributor ? Make your first pull request with us on Pull Request Day - with people around to help you out.

Have a project you've been meaning to contribute to? Make this the day you start. Write some docs, fix a bug, add that feature - save the date and get involved.
